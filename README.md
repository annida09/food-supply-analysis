# 🍽️ Food Supply Planning & Profit Optimization Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes historical transaction data from a food ingredient supplier (Toko A) to support supply planning and profit optimization for Fiscal Year 2026. The analysis focuses on transforming raw transaction data into actionable business insights, including revenue projections and high-margin menu combinations.

---

## Objectives
- Forecast revenue and profit trends for FY2026  
- Identify seasonal patterns in sales performance  
- Optimize product combinations into high-margin menu sets  
- Provide data-driven recommendations for supply and pricing strategy  

---

## Dataset
- Source: Simulated / internal transaction dataset  
- Data type: Item-level sales transactions  
- Scope: Historical data used for forecasting and optimization  

---

## Tools & Methods
- Data Cleaning & Validation (ensuring price and volume consistency)  
- Trend Analysis (MoM revenue & profit projection)  
- Basic Forecasting (seasonality-based projection)  
- Optimization Analysis (menu combination simulation)  

---

## Key Analysis

### FY2026 Financial Projection
- Developed Month-over-Month (MoM) revenue and profit projections  
- Identified peak sales periods driven by seasonal demand  

### Menu Optimization
- Simulated combinations of food items into menu packages  
- Identified top 3 menu sets with the highest profit margins  

---

## Key Insights
- Revenue shows seasonal spikes during high-demand periods (e.g., holidays)  
- Profitability is driven by specific item combinations rather than individual items  
- Bundling products into menu sets significantly increases margin potential  

---

## Recommendations
- Implement bundling strategies to increase average transaction value  
- Introduce bulk purchasing incentives to reduce supply costs  
- Align inventory planning with seasonal demand trends
